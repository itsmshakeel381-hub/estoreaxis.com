About Me

My Story

I started my eCommerce journey as a solo seller trying to figure out Amazon — spending countless hours learning PPC, listing optimization, and account management the hard way. What I discovered was that most sellers were leaving enormous amounts of money on the table not because their products were bad, but because their marketplace management was.

That realization turned into a mission: to give brands the expert, data-driven marketplace management they deserve — without the overhead of a large agency and without the impersonal service that comes with scale. Every client I work with gets direct access to me and the same level of care I'd give my own business.

Today, I lead a focused team of platform specialists through EStoreAxis, managing 150+ brands across 6 major US marketplaces — from first-time Amazon sellers to 7-figure multi-channel brands. We've collectively managed over $9M in ad spend, recovered hundreds of suspended accounts, and generated measurable, repeatable revenue growth for every client we've served.
